"video_id": Unique identifier for each video.
"uploader": Name of the uploader.
"age": Age of the video in days since upload.
"category": Category of the video.
"length": Length of the video in seconds.
"views": View count.
"rating": Average rating (float).
"ratings_count": Number of ratings.
"comments_count": Number of comments.
"related_ids": Array of related video IDs.
"crawl_date": The date when the crawl was performed (extracted from the log file).
